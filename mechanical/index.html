About Us

What do we do?

Our team works with the purpose of bridging academia and industry, giving students of IIT Ropar opportunities to explore and experience roles in Core Mechanical Engineering related roles through internships.

Apart from internships, we also organise industry HR meets, competitions by companies for students of IIT Ropar, and so much more, all with the purpose of healthy interaction between students and industry leaders to enable students to improve their skill set to be up to date with ever changing technologies, and have a better outlook towards corporate life and future prospects.
